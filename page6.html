Recipe Game

Below is an interactive tool to learn my favorite fettucine alfredo recipe. You can cross ingredients you already have off of the list. Then, challenge yourself by putting the steps to the recipe in order. Have fun!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp. butter
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • tsp. salt
  • tsp. pepper
  • bag of spinach
  • cup of heavy whipping cream
  • box fettuccine pasta
  • container club mushrooms
  • Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • cup shaved parmesan
  • Tbsp. lemon juice

Fettuccine Alfredo Recipe Steps

    1. Add butter, garlic, salt, and pepper to pan

    2. Saute but do not burn garlic

    3. Add spinach and heavy whipping cream to pan

    4. Bring to boil then reduce to simmer

    5. Make fettuccine pasta al dente

    6. Saute chopped mushrooms in olive oil

    7. Add 2 Tbsp. pasta water to cream and spinach mixture

    8. Toss pasta and sauce together

    9. Mix in grated parmesan, lemon juice, and mushrooms

    10. Top with shaved parmesan and enjoy!
